Privacy Policy

This page explains how enquiry information is collected, used and shared through the LNT Residence website.

What the form is for

The enquiry form is used to help our team respond with availability, pricing and site-visit options.

Information we collect

  • Name
  • Phone
  • Email
  • Interested in
  • Message

How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry with project availability, pricing and site-visit options.
  • To understand whether you are interested in residential, commercial or retail offerings.
  • To continue communication with you about the specific request you submitted.

How enquiries are handled

Enquiry details are submitted through the website form and routed to the configured response email for follow-up by the team.

This website uses EmailJS to deliver form submissions from the front-end experience to the destination contact email configured for the site.
