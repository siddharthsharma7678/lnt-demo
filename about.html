About Us

L&T Realty is the real estate arm of Larsen and Toubro

The company positions itself among India's leading developers, with residential, commercial and retail projects across major urban markets.

Who they are

L&T Realty states that its portfolio spans around 6.50 million square metres, or about 70 million square feet, across residential, commercial and retail development. It is currently present in Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad and Chennai.

How they describe their work

The public brand message focuses on landmark developments, customer delight at every touch point, design innovation, operational excellence and the use of digitization and new technologies.

CEO message

On the official website, Anupam Kumar, CEO and MD, presents the business as well-positioned for continued growth and expansion because of its premium brand positioning in real estate.

Our lineage

Simple summary of the parent company context shown on the official L&T Realty website.

Larsen and Toubro is described on the site as a USD 23 billion business empire with leadership across technology, engineering, construction, manufacturing and financial services. L&T Realty connects its own identity directly to that larger engineering and governance legacy.
